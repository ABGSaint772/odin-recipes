Baked Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe

Baked Sweet and Sour Chicken is a delicious dish where tender chicken pieces are coated in a crispy, golden breading and then baked to perfection. The star of the dish is the tangy-sweet sauce, made from a blend of ingredients like vinegar, sugar, ketchup, soy sauce, and sometimes pineapple juice, which gives it that signature sweet and sour flavor.
