Pork Sinigang Recipe

Pork Sinigang is a traditional Filipino dish that's loved for its savory, tangy broth and tender pork. It's the ultimate comfort food, especially during rainy days or when you need something to warm you up. The rich, sour broth is the star of the dish, with its perfect balance of tangy, savory, and earthy flavors.
